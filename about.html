About the cafe

ICTCAFE is a virtual directory of ICT references, books, activities, student outputs,
tutorials, and other guides that can help students in their ICT subject.
This website also archives selected student outputs, which others can use
as an example for those who needs ideas in their work.

ICTCAFE is a private repository of works and do not claim any copyrighted material
which may or may not be posted in the future. All student outputs posted have
permission to their respective owner and must be used for educational purposes only.

ICTCAFE will also feature activities in which students can use while they are studying
such as (but not limited to): Online-radio, Video tutorials, etc.
